Mythopoetic Being

The stories we carry become
the lives we live

Each of us carries an intimate, and completely unique, interpretive map of the world and our place within it. This is an intricately personal weave, composed of the stories we have been given — through experience and inheritance.

The waking dream we live within is held together with the mortar of the stories we echo within ourselves, and thus, consequentially, they become the living stories we believe define our lives.

Together, through deep presencing, we examine the myths and the folklore that have been the bones your dissatisfied life has been built upon. We respectfully, and compassionately give them the space for expression, catharsis, meaning, context and resolution.

We transmute the texts, and explore the rewrites that bring you into the life you want to be living. More importantly, my desire is to imbue you with the skills and confidence to navigate this terrain and embrace your life fully — with the power and agency of embodying the temporal Narrative 'I' from a place of gentle equanimity. We do this by connecting with primordial and primal powers for medicine, healing, grace, and guidance — through remembering and recontextualizing the scope of our individuated identities within the epic, infinite and eternal tapestry of life itself.

The Developmental Arc

A 12-session
ceremonial progression

This Arc is a 12-session progression, typically held within a single season — though it can also be expanded into 6, 9, 12, 18, or 24 month commitments for those who want a slower unfold or a deeper dive.

It is built as a continuity container — meaning we don't do the deep ceremonial opening without the steady thread of integration running alongside it. Because once this work begins, it illuminates inner shadows. Not to punish, but to be loved, metabolized, and repatterned.

The Structure

6 Sacred Breath Sessions

3 hours each — deep inner work and ceremonial opening. These are the peak experiences of the arc: full ceremonial containers for expanded states, ancestral contact, and deep somatic release.

6 Guidance & Counsel Sessions

2 hours each — integration, nervous system tending, life-application, and repatterning. These sessions weave the ceremonial insights into the fabric of your daily life.

Recommended pacing: every other week — enough space to integrate, close enough the thread stays warm.

This offering is available as Solo Acolyte Intensives — private, fully customized 3, 6, 9, 12, 18, or 24 month commitments — and as Themed Group Trajectories where the power of collective intention amplifies the personal work.

This is intensive, layered work. It is for those who are seriously committed to self-actualization, spiritual sovereignty, and the kind of healing that changes the shape of a life. If you're looking for a quick fix, this is not the container. If you're ready to go all the way in — this was made for you.

Methodology & Modalities

The sacred tools of
your becoming

Six interwoven modalities form the living fabric of this work — each one a doorway, each one a medicine. Together they create a complete technology of transformation.

01

Entheo-ecology

Turning to the great Mother through animist relational synarchy — Nature's wisdom and medicine. Elemental, ancestral spirit, beings of person and place, plant and animal medicines. Undomesticate your sense of self and return to right relationship with the living world.

02

Embodied Arts

Voice, movement, dance, poetry, and visual art as creative outlets of becoming. Sing through feelings that have no words; move through what the body needs to release; tell your story in embodied form for liberation and self-authorship.

03

Ancestral Divination

Initiated diviner in the Dagara lineage of West Africa, and naturally gifted medium and oracle. Turning to the unseen worlds for clarity and understanding on behalf of communicating spirits — ancestors, elementals, and allies in the great web.

04

Shamanic Bodywork

The body vessel as keeper of truths. Using somatic, therapeutic, shamanic, and energetic modalities to address primary energy centers, held physical tensions, and the deep cellular memory encoded in living flesh and bone.

05

Generative Hypnosis

Communing with the subconscious mind like lucid dreaming — conscious, curious, and free. Engaging the depths and facets of self for innovative solutions from the inherent genius within. Repatterning what no longer serves; seeding what wants to grow.

06

Ritual & Ceremonies

Connecting to the roots of indigeneity within us all. As a medicine woman and lineage-carrying priestess, Adi draws from her medicine basket and spiritual allies to build ritual containers for the development and healing of the whole person.

Methods in Practice

The Ouroboros Cycle

The living spiral of your transformation. Each phase feeds the next, and the cycle returns — deeper each time — as the serpent swallows its own tail and is reborn. This is how change that lasts is made.

I

Preparation & Priming

Connect with and cultivate intentions through lessons, assignments, and personal sessions. We prime the psyche — addressing inherited blueprints, identifying limiting beliefs and emotional wounds that have been running the show. We build a foundation of somatic and embodied practices to support peak emotional states and readiness for the deeper work ahead.

II

Transformation & Alchemy

The peak — ritual and medicine ceremonies. We access extraordinary states of consciousness and bring all modalities together in service to the transformation at hand. We turn to the realms of Spirit through Earth-based modalities — divinations, channeling, ritual, offerings, prayer, and the cultivation of spiritual fortitude. This is where the mythic becomes personal, and the personal becomes mythic.

III

Grounding & Integration

The recombobulation phase. Proper aftercare for lasting positive impact. We tend to what's been revealed — carefully, devotedly. We intensively integrate new awarenesses and ground the changes that have emerged from our intentions. This is where the medicine becomes your life, and the transformation becomes liveable.

∞

The cycle returns — each revolution deeper, each season more alive.
