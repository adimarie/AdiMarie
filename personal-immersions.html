Personal Immersions

Extended containers for those ready to go all the way in.

The Offering

Radical presence.
Spiritual reclamation.

A Personal Immersion is exactly what it sounds like: a full, complete submersion in the work. One to five days of continuous ceremonial space — held with extraordinary care, calibrated entirely to your threshold, and oriented toward the kind of transformation that you can feel in your bones for the rest of your life.

These are bespoke experiences. No two are the same. They are designed in conversation with you before they begin — drawing from your specific situation, your edges, your longings, and your resistances — and then held with total devotion as they unfold. Adi Marie will be your guide, your witness, and your medicine person throughout.

Immersions typically include a combination of Shamanic Bodywork, Sacred Breath sessions, Ancestral Divinations, ceremonial work, time in the land, and integration sessions. They are held in sacred space in Topanga Canyon, or — for those called to a particular place — at a location of significance to your journey.

Sometimes the most loving thing you can give yourself is an uninterrupted season of going in. Not checking the phone, not managing the day, not performing the life — just going in, all the way, with someone skilled enough to hold the edges. That is what an Immersion offers.

Immersions are for those at genuine thresholds — standing before a major life change, in the aftermath of significant loss, or simply ready for the kind of deep reset that a week of vacation cannot provide. They are for those who have tried many things and are ready for something that will actually reach the root.

What a Personal Immersion May Include

Sacred Breath Sessions

Deep ceremonial openings for expanded states and inner work.

Shamanic Bodywork

Hands-on somatic and energetic healing sessions.

Ancestral Divination

Readings and transmissions for clarity and direction.

Land Connection

Time in Topanga Canyon's sacred landscape for grounding and medicine.

Ritual & Ceremony

Custom ceremonial containers woven through the immersion.

Integration Sessions

Guidance and counsel sessions to weave the insights into life.

Duration ranges from 1 to 5 days. All immersions are designed from scratch through a detailed pre-immersion consultation. Post-immersion follow-up sessions are included.
