Sliding Scale

Sacred Reciprocity

Where the personal becomes communal, and healing becomes a circle.

The Practice of Fair Exchange

Healing belongs to the whole community

In traditional ways, healers were supported by the community because the health of one is the health of all. That truth still guides my work.

My offerings require a deep investment of energy, time, and training, and I am committed to promoting economic justice. We do not all walk the same path of resources.

I believe that healing and spiritual counsel should be accessible — while honoring the real costs of sustaining this work.

Choose Your Tier

Three pathways of reciprocity

I do not require income verification. I trust you to choose honestly.

Sponsorship / Pay-It-Forward

Abundance Rate

Reflects the actual value of my work and helps fund lower-cost sessions for those in hardship.

This tier is for you if you:

  • ◈ Easily meet basic needs
  • ◈ Own property, have savings, or expendable income
  • ◈ Can afford wants, travel, and investments
  • ◈ Wish to support community healing

Choosing this tier is a gift to the community. Thank you.

Stewardship

Standard Rate

Reflects fair, sustainable compensation for the work.

This tier is for you if you:

  • ◈ Meet basic needs with some ease
  • ◈ Occasionally stretch your budget, but avoid hardship
  • ◈ Have a steady income and some expendable funds

This covers my operating costs, expenses, materials, and a fair living wage.

Scholarship

Accessibility Rate

Acknowledges financial hardship and systemic barriers.

This tier is for you if you:

  • ◈ Often worry about basic needs
  • ◈ Have little expendable income
  • ◈ Qualify for assistance or live paycheck-to-paycheck
  • ◈ Would face hardship paying the full cost

If this rate is still out of reach, please email me to discuss alternative options, including payment plans.

Reflection

The Spirit of Economic Equanimity

This work is not just a transaction. It is a sacred exchange.

Please reflect honestly:

Does paying the full rate feel like a sacrifice — or genuine hardship?

Could you pay more to support someone else's healing?

Would paying less help you access this work without sacrificing essentials?

Whatever tier you choose, you are welcome here.
Together, we keep the circle strong.
