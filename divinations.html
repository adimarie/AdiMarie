The Offering

Clarity from the
other side of the veil

The ancestral and elemental realms are not distant or mythological. They are alive — present in the patterns of our lives, in the recurring themes, the unexplained pulls, the inexplicable resistances. A divination is a technology for listening to what those realms are saying — and for receiving the guidance that the living world is always offering, if we have the tools to hear it.

Adi Marie is an initiated diviner in the Dagara tradition of West Africa — a lineage that carries ancient, tested technologies for ancestral contact, elemental medicine, and the reading of the forces that shape a life. She is also a naturally gifted medium and oracle, with an innate capacity to receive and transmit communication from the unseen worlds that predates and sits alongside her formal training.

Her readings are specific. They are not vague or performative. They speak to the actual patterns and forces at work in your life — naming what is there, offering perspective from the ancestral and elemental viewpoints, and pointing toward the kinds of tending and action that will bring greater alignment and clarity.

The ancestors are not silent. The elements are not indifferent. They have been trying to reach you. A divination creates the conditions for that contact — and for receiving what they've been trying to say. — Adi Marie

Types of Readings

Ancestral Divination

Drawing from the Dagara lineage tradition, these readings open a channel to the ancestral field — addressing what your lineage is asking of you, what is trying to heal, and what gifts and burdens have been transmitted through the blood. Particularly powerful for those working with ancestral trauma, lineage healing, or questions of identity and belonging.

Elemental Divination

A reading of the elemental forces at work in your life — Water, Fire, Earth, Mineral, and Nature — and how their balance or imbalance is expressing in your current situation. Useful for understanding what element is calling for attention and what offerings, practices, or life adjustments will restore the flow.

Oracle & Mediumship

Adi's natural mediumistic gifts allow for direct communication with those who have passed, spirit guides, and other entities in the unseen world. These sessions are less structured — responsive to what arises, directed by who and what shows up to speak.

All sessions include time for questions and integration conversation. Recordings are available. Sessions are typically 90 minutes to 2 hours.

Divinations are available virtually worldwide and in person in Topanga Canyon. They are also frequently integrated into Personal Immersions and the Developmental Arc.
