Collective Healing

Ceremonial & Ritual Groups

Because the work of tending the life web is communal.

The Offering

The power of
collective intention

There are things that can only happen in community. The individual work is essential — it is the root. But when people come together in ceremony with genuine intention, something amplifies. The field between people becomes a medicine in itself, and the collective healing that emerges often goes beyond what any individual could access alone.

Ceremonial & Ritual Groups at The Animist Apothecary take several forms. Themed trajectory programs — typically 4 to 12 sessions — where a consistent group moves through a shared arc of transformation together. Seasonal ceremonies — single gatherings aligned to the turning points of the year, open to community. And ceremonial training intensives for those developing their own facilitation practice.

Each group is a held container — with clear agreements, skilled facilitation, and the same devotion to sacred space that Adi brings to all of her work. These are not casual gatherings. They are ceremonial. They are intentional. And they are designed to leave a mark.

When a group of people gathers in genuine ceremony — with their whole selves, their real questions, their actual grief and longing — something happens that can't be manufactured or explained. It is one of the most powerful medicines I know. — Adi Marie

Types of Group Offerings

Themed Group Trajectories

4–12 session programs for consistent groups moving through a shared arc. Current and upcoming themes include: The Underworld & Return, Ancestral Healing for Modern People, Embodied Sovereignty, and The Mythic Body. Groups meet every 2–4 weeks with integration practices between sessions.

Seasonal Ceremonies

Ceremonies aligned to the solstices, equinoxes, and significant turning points of the seasonal wheel. These are single-session community gatherings, open enrollment, and designed to mark the threshold moments of the year with intentional practice and collective ritual.

Eco-Immersion Groups

Ceremonial gatherings in nature — on the land, at the water, in the hills of Topanga or at sacred sites further afield. Oriented toward service to the living world: attending to the spirits of place, tending the ecological grid, and deepening our felt sense of belonging to the earth community.

Private Group Engagements

Custom ceremonial programs for organizations, communities of practice, and intentional groups. Available for companies, therapy collectives, medicine communities, and other groups seeking ceremonial culture as part of their organizational or community life.
