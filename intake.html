Agreements & Consent

Thank you for taking the time to step toward this work, and for your willingness to enter into something both delicate and potent. The questions that follow are an invitation to pause, to breathe, and to reflect on the many layers of your life — body, heart, mind, spirit, and the web you live within.

There are no "perfect" answers here; only what feels true for you in this moment, in whatever language comes most naturally.

Practicals

The basics — how to reach you, and a little context about where you are in your life right now.

Emergency & Safety

This information helps me hold your care with the fullness it deserves. It stays confidential and is only referenced if needed for your safety.

Name and phone number of someone I can reach in case of emergency.

Include plant, food, environmental, and medication allergies.

Anything current or historical that would be important for me to know — surgeries, diagnoses, chronic conditions.

Body & Physical Health

Your body carries the story of your life. This section helps me understand where you are physically — not to diagnose, but to listen.

How does your body feel day to day? Any areas of tension, chronic pain, fatigue, or vitality?

Touch sensitivities, temperature preferences, claustrophobia, scent sensitivities, etc.

Mind & Emotional Landscape

This section is about your inner weather — the emotional and psychological terrain you are navigating right now. There is no wrong answer. Speak as gently or directly as you need.

What is alive in you emotionally right now? What are you carrying?

Any diagnoses, therapeutic relationships, hospitalizations, or significant mental health experiences — past or present.

Anything I should be aware of that could be activating — sounds, topics, touch, imagery, etc.

Therapist, community, spiritual director, support group, trusted friends — who holds you?

Spirit & Intention

This is the heart of why you are here. Speak freely — in poetry, in fragments, in full sentences. Whatever comes.

What traditions, practices, or spiritual landscapes have shaped you? What is your relationship to spirit, nature, or the sacred?

Any experience with bodywork, shamanic or ceremonial work, somatic therapy, plant medicine, meditation, or other healing modalities?

What brings you here? What are you hoping to explore, heal, remember, or release?

Anything I haven't asked that you want me to know — about you, your life, your hopes, your fears, or your needs.

Thank you for sharing yourself so fully.

Your intake has been received. I will review everything you've shared with the care and attention it deserves. You'll hear from me soon to discuss next steps and schedule our first session together.

In the meantime, take a breath. The hardest part — the stepping forward — is already done.
